## Interpretation

The multiplier and counterfactual penalty operate correctly, but the shielded
training configuration does not produce an intrinsically safe policy within
twenty updates.

The proposed episode cost remains more than three times the configured limit.
Unshielded safety cost remains above the Stage 3D baseline, while hazard recall
decreases substantially.

The likely cause is the on-policy action-transition mismatch introduced by
runtime action replacement. PPO records the proposed policy action, but the
observed next state is generated by the shield replacement action.

The next calibration therefore uses transition-aligned unshielded Lagrangian
training. The semantic shield remains available for separate deployment
evaluation.
